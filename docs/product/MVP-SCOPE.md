# Deployable MVP Scope

## Implemented

- Premium responsive dashboard with financial summaries and AI-personalized recommendations.
- Wallet management demo with wallet creation, funding simulation, balances, and controls.
- Payment preparation, approval-policy visibility, queue tracking, and payment detail review.
- Transaction search, filtering, detail inspection, and CSV export.
- Exchange-rate monitoring, currency conversion simulation, trend visualization, and rate alerts.
- Specialized AI-agent workspace with Cashflow, Payment, Risk, and FX agents.
- Profile, preference, security, and notification settings.
- Command palette, notification drawer, mobile navigation, PWA manifest, health endpoint, CI, and Vercel deployment guidance.

## Demonstration constraints

The MVP uses illustrative local data. It does not connect to banks, payment processors, identity providers, live FX markets, databases, or production AI models. Demonstration actions do not move money or create external accounts.

## Production work still requiring external services

- Authentication and identity verification.
- PostgreSQL persistence and immutable double-entry ledger.
- Payment-rail and mobile-money providers.
- Live FX quotes and quote locking.
- KYC, AML, sanctions, and fraud providers.
- Real AI provider integration, evaluation, permissions, and audit trails.
- Native mobile application distribution and store credentials.

The repository is a complete deployable product demonstration, not a licensed production financial institution.

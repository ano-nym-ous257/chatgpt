# PaymentFlow AI — M2 Ledger Engine Specification

Status: draft specification; not implemented.

This document is intended for review by financial architects, auditors, and senior engineers. M2 depends on the future completion and acceptance of M0 (Foundation) and M1 (Authentication and Identity); their draft specifications do not indicate that those milestones are complete or implemented.

---

## SECTION 1 — Milestone Goals
- **Objectives**: Implement an immutable, double‑entry ledger as the financial backbone.
- **Deliverables**: Ledger accounts, journal entries, posting engine, balance engine, reconciliation, APIs, audit trail.
- **Dependencies**: M0 infra, M1 identity/authentication.
- **Business Value**: Provides single source of truth for all financial activity.
- **Risks**: Incorrect postings, concurrency issues, reconciliation failures.
- **Definition of Done**: Ledger APIs operational, balances derived from postings, reconciliation passes.
- **Acceptance Criteria**: All tests in Section 17 succeed.

---

## SECTION 2 — Accounting Principles
- **Double‑entry**: Every debit has equal credit.
- **No orphan entries**: Journal entries must have ≥2 lines.
- **No direct balance updates**: Balances derived from postings only.
- **Immutable postings**: Journal entries cannot be altered once committed.
- **Audit trail**: All changes logged.
- **Model**:
  - **Journal Entry**: Header record.
  - **Journal Entry Lines**: Debit/Credit lines.
  - **Ledger Accounts**: Chart of Accounts.
  - **Balances**: Derived from journal lines.

---

## SECTION 3 — Chart of Accounts
- **Categories**:
  - Assets (Cash, Accounts Receivable)
  - Liabilities (Accounts Payable, Loans)
  - Equity (Capital, Retained Earnings)
  - Revenue (Fees, FX gains)
  - Expenses (Operating, FX losses)
  - Off‑Balance (Suspense, Reserve)
- **System Accounts**: Settlement, Fee, FX, Suspense.
- **Numbering**: 1000–1999 Assets, 2000–2999 Liabilities, 3000–3999 Equity, 4000–4999 Revenue, 5000–5999 Expenses.
- **Hierarchy**: Parent/child accounts for grouping.

---

## SECTION 4 — Database Implementation
Tables:
- **ledger_accounts**: id, type, currency, parent_id.
- **journal_entries**: id, timestamp, batch_id, immutable flag.
- **journal_entry_lines**: id, entry_id, account_id, debit, credit.
- **ledger_balances**: account_id, balance_type, amount, currency.
- **posting_batches**: id, status, created_at.
- **exchange_rates**: currency_pair, rate, timestamp.
- **reconciliation_records**: id, type, status, created_at.
- **ledger_snapshots**: id, timestamp, balances.
- **balance_checkpoints**: id, account_id, checkpoint_balance.
- **financial_periods**: id, start_date, end_date, closed flag.
- **Indexes**: account_id, entry_id, batch_id.
- **Constraints**: debit = credit per entry.
- **Partitioning**: journal_entry_lines partitioned by month.
- **Retention**: snapshots retained, raw entries immutable.

---

## SECTION 5 — Posting Engine
- **Atomic transactions**: ACID compliance.
- **Idempotency**: Posting key ensures no duplicates.
- **Concurrency safety**: Row‑level locks.
- **Rollback**: Entire batch rollback on failure.
- **Batch posting**: Grouped entries processed together.
- **Workflow**: Validate → Create journal entry → Insert lines → Commit → Update balances.

---

## SECTION 6 — Financial Events
Examples:
- **Deposit**: Debit Cash, Credit Settlement.
- **Withdrawal**: Debit Settlement, Credit Cash.
- **Transfer**: Debit Source, Credit Destination.
- **Payment**: Debit Customer, Credit Merchant.
- **Invoice Payment**: Debit Accounts Payable, Credit Cash.
- **Payroll**: Debit Payroll Expense, Credit Cash.
- **Currency Conversion**: Debit FX Loss, Credit FX Gain.
- **Fee Collection**: Debit Customer, Credit Fee Revenue.
- **Refund**: Reverse original posting.
- **Chargeback**: Debit Merchant, Credit Customer.
- **Adjustment/Reversal**: Correction entries with audit trail.

---

## SECTION 7 — Balance Engine
- **Types**: Current, Available, Pending, Reserved.
- **Snapshots**: Daily balance snapshots.
- **Rebuild**: Recalculate from journal lines.
- **Algorithms**:
  - Current = Σ(debits − credits).
  - Available = Current − Reserved.
  - Historical = Snapshot + Δ(postings since snapshot).

---

## SECTION 8 — Multi‑Currency Accounting
- **Supported**: USD, EUR, GBP, GHS, NGN, KES.
- **Precision**: Currency‑specific decimals.
- **Exchange rates**: Stored in `exchange_rates`.
- **FX entries**: Debit FX Loss, Credit FX Gain.
- **Workflow**: Convert → Post FX gain/loss → Update balances.
- **Rounding**: Bankers’ rounding.

---

## SECTION 9 — Reconciliation
- **Daily reconciliation**: Compare balances vs postings.
- **Internal**: Cross‑check subsystems.
- **External**: Match provider statements.
- **Exception handling**: Suspense accounts.
- **Workflow**: Extract → Compare → Flag → Resolve.

---

## SECTION 10 — Reversals & Adjustments
- **Reversal**: Create equal/opposite entry.
- **Correction**: Adjustment entries.
- **Backdated corrections**: Allowed with audit trail.
- **Period protection**: Closed periods immutable.

---

## SECTION 11 — Ledger API
Endpoints:
- `POST /ledger/post` → Create posting.
- `GET /ledger/accounts` → List accounts.
- `GET /ledger/accounts/{id}` → Account details.
- `GET /ledger/journal-entries` → List entries.
- `GET /ledger/journal-entries/{id}` → Entry details.
- `GET /ledger/balances` → Balances.
- `POST /ledger/reconcile` → Trigger reconciliation.
- `POST /ledger/reverse` → Reverse entry.
- **Validation**: Debit=Credit, account existence.
- **Authorization**: RBAC enforced.
- **Idempotency**: Posting key required.

---

## SECTION 12 — Security
- **Double posting prevention**: Idempotency keys.
- **Replay protection**: Nonce/timestamp.
- **Tampering prevention**: Immutable entries.
- **Unauthorized postings**: RBAC enforced.
- **Race conditions**: Locks.
- **Duplicate detection**: Unique constraints.

---

- **Posting history**: Full trace.
- **User attribution**: Who initiated.
- **Service attribution**: Which service posted.
- **Change history**: Adjustments logged.
- **Reports**: Exportable audit logs.
- **Regulatory traceability**: SOX/IFRS compliance.

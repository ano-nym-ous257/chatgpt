Developer handoff specifications

TokensUse CSS custom properties mirrored from design-tokens.json.
AccessibilityEvery actionable control needs visible focus, semantic labels, and keyboard support.
Data densityFinancial tables should preserve numeric alignment and status chips at all widths.
MotionUse subtle transforms and opacity. Honor prefers-reduced-motion.
ChartsPair color with labels and values. Do not rely on color alone for risk or status.
